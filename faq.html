1. How is the shipping fee calculated ?

The shipping cost of an order is calculated based on 3 factors:

• Total volume of orders: calculated based on the volume of products created by the Seller

• Distance between Seller and Buyer: calculated based on Seller's default pickup address and Buyer's shipping address

• Order value: if the order value is large, the shipping fee will include shipping insurance.

Different shipping units will have different shipping rates because each unit has its own fee policy.


Note:

• The shipping fee will be automatically updated for the activated shipping unit based on the product volume
• Different carriers will have different shipping charges.
